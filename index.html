PotiNeuro 2026

1st Systems Neuroscience International Symposium

Natal · Rio Grande do Norte · Brazil

About

We will bring together leading researchers in systems neuroscience from around the world to present their current work and engage in discussion in an informal two-day programme.

The symposium will take place in the auditorium of the Brain Institute at UFRN in August 2026 (specific dates to be confirmed). The event is open to the public, and everyone is welcome.

We will also invite contributions from local researchers. If you would like to propose a talk, please write to propose@potineuro.ufrn.br .
